Overview

Anime is a diverse form of animation originating from Japan, characterized by vibrant art, fantastical

themes, and unique storytelling. It covers a wide range of genres, from action and adventure to romance

and slice-of-life, appealing to various age groups and tastes. Over the years, anime has grown into a global

cultural phenomenon, influencing art, fashion, and entertainment worldwide.

Fun Facts

    The term "anime" is a shortened, Japanized pronunciation of the word "animation" and has been around since the early 1900s. The first verifiable Japanese animated film was made in 1917.
    The longest-running anime is Sazae-san with more than 7500 episodes.
    In Japan, more paper is used to print manga than toilet paper!
